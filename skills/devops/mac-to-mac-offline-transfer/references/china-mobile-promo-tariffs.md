### Activation flow

2. Click "→→→点此申请办理" → redirects to 3.kazhijia.cn (seller storefront)
3. Search by tariff name or browse list

5. Courier delivers SIM → pay 50-100元 (first top-up) → self-activate

### Foreign passport problem
- Online promo tariffs typically require 中国身份证 (Chinese ID card)
- Foreign nationals (护照) must verify in physical 营业厅 (China Mobile office)
- Strategy: bring the simkazhijia URL/bookmark to the office and ask them to activate on passport
- Alternative: ask online seller's WeChat customer service if 护照 is accepted

### Tmall / JD approach
- Official China Mobile stores on Tmall (zhongguoyidong.tmall.com) and JD
- Higher prices, worse tariffs — not worth it for promo deals
- Both platforms require login + may ask for account binding

### Key insight
The 19-29元 tariffs with 185-240GB are REAL (not scam) — they work through
a carrier subsidy model (12-month discount period). After 12 months they
revert to ~39-59元 base plan. Cancel before renewal or let it revert.
