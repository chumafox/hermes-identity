## Key Distinctions

| Term | Meaning | Relevance |
|------|---------|-----------|
| 全国通用流量 | National traffic (works anywhere in China) | **Required** for inter-province travel |

## Ordering Flow

1. Find plan on simkazhijia.com or similar aggregator
2. Click "点此申请办理" → opens order form (usually WeChat-based)
3. Fill: name, ID number, delivery address
4. SIM arrives by courier (1-3 days)
5. Pay initial top-up to courier (快递处首充50元) — required to unlock promo
6. Courier assists with activation, or self-activate via China Mobile app
7. Promo traffic kicks in within 48 hours

## Pitfalls

- **Stale listings**: Third-party sites may list plans that expired months ago. Always check the publish date on the article. Plans from Oct 2024 may no longer be orderable.
- **定向流量 ≠ 通用流量**: "30G定向" only works on listed apps. Don't count it as general-purpose data.
- **合约期**: Most plans have 12-24 month contracts. Cancelling early may forfeit the rebate.
- **归属地**: The phone number's 归属地 (home city) determines which local promos apply. A 上海-number plan ships anywhere but the number stays 上海.
- **老用户不能办理**: These promos are for new subscribers (新入网) only. You cannot switch an existing plan to this tariff.
- **Price-per-GB trap**: Promo plans are dramatically cheaper than standard postpaid (0.10元/GB vs 1.99元/GB for 全球通5GA). If you need lots of data, getting a secondary SIM on a promo plan is vastly cheaper than upgrading your main plan.
